date: 2022-01-02
---

Today I picked up following Russian words of common trees (дерево) in Finland (and Russia):

* Birch tree is [берёза](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%D0%B1%D0%B5%D1%80%D1%91%D0%B7%D0%B0#Russian)
* Pine tree is сосна
* Spruce is ель
* Aspen is осина
* Fir is пихта
